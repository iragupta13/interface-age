The Interface Age

Visual Directions

Three sample moods for the site before we apply a full redesign. Each one shows a different visual language for the same neurotech guide: more atlas-like, more lab-like, or more archival.

Direction 01

Neural Atlas

Elegant, technical, and spacious. It feels like a high-end map of a frontier field rather than a startup landing page.

Best for: clarity, beauty, and serious longform reading.

Real-page moves: contour motifs, signal arcs, wider visual bands, cleaner captions, and premium field-guide typography.

Direction 02

Interface Lab

Sharper, denser, and more futuristic. It feels like a live systems console for an industry that is becoming real.

Direction 03

Black Site Archive

Research-dossier energy with a hidden-program atmosphere. It feels immersive, deep, and slightly forbidden without becoming gimmicky.

Best for: narrative depth, mystery, and archival seriousness.

Real-page moves: dossier framing, stamped metadata, layered records, and research pages that feel like recovered documents.
